Your Blender models can be put on your webpages. There seems to be a number of ways.
  • Blender4web Download, extract and setup within blender and you can export your model as a single webpage.
    Export Web player



    Link to page e.g. suzanne.html
    Or include as iframe 
    <iframe src="suzanne.html">

  • SKETCHLAB

    https://sketchfab.com/ will host your files and you can export directly from blender
    https://sketchfab.com/exporters/blender
